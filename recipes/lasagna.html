Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with a rich meat sauce,
creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. It's baked until bubbly and golden,
making it a hearty and comforting meal perfect for gatherings or family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 jar (about 700g) tomato pasta sauce
  • 250g ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 200g shredded mozzarella
  • Salt, pepper, and oil

Instructions

  1. Boil noodles – Cook them in salted water, then drain.
  2. Cook meat – Brown the beef in a pan with a little oil. Add salt, pepper, and the pasta sauce. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Layer it – In a baking dish:
    • A little meat sauce
    • 3 noodles
    • Some ricotta
    • Some meat sauce
    • Some mozzarella
    • Repeat 2 more times
  4. Bake – Cover with foil and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake 15 more minutes.
  5. Rest – Let it sit for 10 minutes before cutting.
